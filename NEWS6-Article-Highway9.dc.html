One Killed in Highway 9 Crash Minutes After Overnight Tremors
By NEWS 6 Newsroom Staff | Filed 6:05 AM, June 28, 2003
FILE PHOTO — HIGHWAY 9 OVERPASS, EMERGENCY RESPONSE
One person is dead and another was taken to County General with non-life-threatening injuries following a two-vehicle collision near the Highway 9 overpass in the predawn hours Saturday.

The crash occurred just before 4:50 a.m. — within minutes of the seismic activity felt across the region overnight, according to timestamps from dispatch logs. Investigators have attributed the collision to wet pavement and reduced visibility.

"The whole road just kind of dropped for a second," said one motorist who arrived at the scene shortly after emergency crews. "I don't know how else to describe it."

The highway was closed for several hours while crews cleared the scene. The victim's identity is being withheld pending notification of family. The surviving driver remains in stable condition.

County officials say there is no confirmed connection between the crash and the tremors reported elsewhere in tonight's broadcast.
