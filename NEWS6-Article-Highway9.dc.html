Two Injured in Two-Vehicle Collision on Highway 9
By NEWS 6 Newsroom Staff | Filed 5:32 PM, June 27, 2003
[ FILE PHOTO — HIGHWAY 9 OVERPASS, EMERGENCY RESPONSE ]
Two people were taken to County General with non-life-threatening injuries following a collision near the Highway 9 overpass Friday afternoon.

Police say wet road conditions may have contributed to the crash. The highway was closed for roughly ninety minutes while crews cleared the scene.

Both drivers were treated at the scene before transport. Names have not been released pending notification of family. The cause remains under investigation by county traffic division.

Motorists are advised to use caution on Highway 9 through the weekend as minor debris cleanup continues.
