Cargo Ship Vanishes Off Coast; No Distress Signal Sent
By NEWS 6 Newsroom Staff | Filed 11:47 PM, June 27, 2003
FILE PHOTO — COAST GUARD / VESSEL LAST KNOWN POSITION
A cargo vessel sank off the coast late Friday night in what officials are calling an unusual and still-unexplained incident. Local fishermen observing the ship through binoculars reported that it went under in less than a minute.

No distress call was received from the vessel. Coast Guard radio contact could not be established either before or after the sinking. Rescue crews were dispatched to the area but have not yet located wreckage or survivors.

Approximately five hours after the ship went down, the surrounding region experienced seismic activity strong enough to damage several buildings in nearby coastal towns. Seismologists have not commented on any possible connection between the two events.

NEWS 6 aired a special emergency broadcast covering both developments as they happened.
