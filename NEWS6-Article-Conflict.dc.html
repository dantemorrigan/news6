Diplomats Push for Ceasefire as Overseas Conflict Enters Third Week
By NEWS 6 Wire Services | Filed 2:05 PM, June 27, 2003
International officials met for a second round of talks Friday in an effort to broker a temporary ceasefire in the ongoing overseas conflict.

A spokesperson described the talks as "constructive but incomplete," with both sides said to be weighing proposed terms over the weekend.

Humanitarian groups have called for safe corridors to be established regardless of the outcome. A follow-up session is expected early next week, though officials cautioned that no timeline for a resolution has been set.
