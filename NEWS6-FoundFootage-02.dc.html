NEWS 6: Found Footage — Lost Boys
By NEWS 6 Newsroom Staff | Filed 11:05 AM, July 5, 2003
⚠ RECOVERED CAMCORDER FOOTAGE. ORIGIN UNKNOWN. NEVER VERIFIED OR AIRED BY NEWS 6.
This footage was submitted to NEWS 6 anonymously. It appears to show two young boys entering wooded terrain near Blackmoor Cove, reportedly searching the area on their own. NEWS 6 has been unable to identify either child or determine when the recording was made.

No missing persons matching this description have been reported to local authorities. NEWS 6 has not verified the authenticity of this recording and does not endorse its content.
video_archive // found_070503.rec // source unconfirmed
The recording runs barely twenty seconds. In that time, the two boys can be heard discussing plans to search the tree line for what one of them refers to only as "monsters." One boy carries the camera; the other moves ahead, in and out of frame.

Within seconds, the boy holding the camera calls out and whistles for his companion, apparently believing him to be following close behind. What steps into frame in his place is not identified anywhere in the recording. The camera is dropped, and the footage cuts out almost immediately after.

NEWS 6 has not been able to determine what, if anything, appears in the final seconds of the footage.
